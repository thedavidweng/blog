## Conclusion

In 2023, using a GPT-4-level model cost $20 per month.

By 2026, open models' capabilities are approaching the top-tier models from the big three and are available for free, and domestic tech giants are also distributing tokens through limited-time events and developer programs. During this intense competition, being a regular consumer is truly the best time.

Since I started using AI to write code in March this year, my actual spending has only been U$20.81 (excluding Coze Space), which is absolutely negligible compared to the value it has created for me.
