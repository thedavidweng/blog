This article lists affordable solutions for reproducing the looks from Kamen Rider The series and Shin Kamen Rider that started from a Tieba post, with different attempt combinations. The characteristic is to use common off-the-shelf products (mainly Pinduoduo, Taobao, with Goofish as supplementary) as much as possible. Products that are completely Kamen Rider might only be the helmet and belt.
