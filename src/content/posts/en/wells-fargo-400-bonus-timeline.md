Wells Fargo's $400 sign-up bonus requires $3,000 in direct deposits within 90 days. I opened this account on the same trip as U.S. Bank — drove from Vancouver to Seattle on July 23 and hit two branches in one afternoon.

Background conditions were the same as the previous post: ITIN, a friend's Bellevue address, a SoFi account, and a U.S. bank statement as proof of address. Fake DD method referenced from Doctor of Credit's DD data collection page.

[Doctor of Credit: List Of Methods Banks Count As Direct Deposits](https://www.doctorofcredit.com/knowledge-base/list-methods-banks-count-direct-deposits/)

## July 23 · In-Branch Account Opening

Opened the account at a Wells Fargo branch in Bellevue. Deposited $25 on the spot.

## July 25 · SoFi → Wells Fargo

Initiated an ACH transfer of $3,000 from SoFi to Wells Fargo. Upon arrival, it was recognized as a direct deposit. First fake DD done.

## Aug 8 · SoFi → Wells Fargo

Sent a second ACH transfer of $2,000 from SoFi to Wells Fargo.

## Aug 18 · U.S. Bank → Wells Fargo

Initiated an ACH transfer of $501 from the U.S. Bank account opened during the same trip.

## Oct 24 · Bonus Arrived

$400 sign-up bonus landed.
