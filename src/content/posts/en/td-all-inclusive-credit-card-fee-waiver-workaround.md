TD All-Inclusive Banking Plan's monthly fee is $30.95, waived if you maintain a daily closing balance of $6,000 or more[^1]. One of its key perks: an annual fee rebate on one eligible TD credit card, up to $139 (primary cardholder) + $75 (additional cardholder)[^1]. The problem: $6,000 sitting in a chequing account earns no interest, and the opportunity cost usually exceeds the rebate.

## Changing Account Types Online

Both TD EasyWeb and the TD App support self-serve account type changes. Requests are typically reviewed within two business days, and the change does not affect your account number or debit card.

## How to Do It

In the month your credit card annual fee is charged, upgrade your chequing account to All-Inclusive. Once the rebate lands, downgrade back to a lower-tier chequing account. You only need to maintain the $6,000 balance for one month a year.

Steps: EasyWeb → select account → Manage → Change Account Type → select All-Inclusive Banking Plan → submit. Same flow on the TD App: Accounts → select account → Manage → Change Account Type.

Downgrade follows the exact same steps — just select a lower-tier chequing account as the target.
